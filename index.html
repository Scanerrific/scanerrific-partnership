09 Sample dispatch & tracking

Every sample has a QR code and a story.

The bakery sample lifecycle is short and brutal — one lost package can push a retailer trial by weeks, and a fresh bake can't wait. Scanerrific labels every sample at dispatch and follows it until the buyer's feedback lands back on the record.

  • QR labels in A4, 6×4", and 85×35mm formats
  • Dispatch → arrival → feedback, one thread
  • Inline messaging on every order — no email needed
  • Activity timeline: complete visual history
  • Native iOS & Android app for scanning on the go
  • Address book with pre-filled order forms

10 Trial planning & shelf life

Countdown clocks, ready before the auditor asks.

For bakery, shelf life is the whole game — a study that isn't documented is a study that didn't happen. Scanerrific builds the paper trail as you work, not the night before an audit. Trial plans keep you honest about what still needs testing, and by when.
