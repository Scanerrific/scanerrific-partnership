02 The challenge

Short shelf life. Tight margins. Too many spreadsheets.

For a bakery producing short-shelf-life products, every minute matters. Materials arrive from suppliers, need labelling, get scheduled into trials, and must be tracked through every stage — or they expire before they're used. The cost of lost time isn't just money. It's missed launch windows, rushed audits, and a team that's always firefighting instead of developing.

  • Supplier materials arriving without clear batch data
  • Labelling done manually — slow and error-prone
  • Trial plans buried in email threads
  • Materials lost in freezers or long-life storage
  • Samples dispatched with no tracking on cost or volume
  • Organoleptic data scattered across paper forms

This is what every bakery NPD team we've worked with describes. The chaos isn't the exception — it's the default.

03 Key suspect

Getting materials from suppliers — the first break point.

The moment a pallet arrives from your supplier is where most traceability gaps begin. Missing lot numbers, unclear allergen data, no expiry date recorded. By the time the material reaches the bakery floor, half the information you need is already gone. Scanerrific captures it at goods-in — before it becomes someone else's problem tomorrow.

  • Batch intake with lot number, supplier, date received
  • Allergen & dietary flags recorded at point of entry
  • Expiry & shelf-life data captured immediately
  • Storage location assigned — freezer, chill, ambient
  • QR label printed on receipt for instant tracking
  • Supplier history searchable by ingredient or batch

10 Disposal & shelf life

Know when to keep, when to taste, and when to let go.

In short-shelf-life production, a sample that isn't tested in time is a wasted batch. A sample that sits past its disposal date can ruin an audit. Scanerrific tracks every sample's shelf-life window and disposal date — colour-coded by urgency. You'll never again find a forgotten sample at the back of a freezer.

  • Disposal dates set per sample at creation
  • Colour-coded urgency: Critical / Warning / Recent
  • Automated countdown clocks on every sample
  • Bulk disposal actions for expired samples
  • Clear audit trail: who disposed what and when
  • Housekeeping reports — know your freezer inventory

11 Storage tracking

Freezer, chill, or ambient — know what's where, always.

A bakery runs on multiple storage environments. Frozen ingredients for long-life planning. Chilled materials for short-term trials. Ambient for dry goods. Without a system, each zone becomes a black hole. Scanerrific maps every material to its storage location — so you can see at a glance what's in the freezer, what's approaching its window, and what needs to move next.

  • Storage locations mapped hierarchically (zone → shelf → bin)
  • Freezer, chill, and ambient zones tracked separately
  • Material age visible per location
  • Long-life stock rotation tracked by date received
  • QR scan moves materials between locations
  • Inventory snapshot at any point in time

12 Sample shipping

Every sample sent. Every cost tracked. No more surprise courier bills.

Sample shipping is one of the biggest hidden costs in NPD. How much did your team spend on couriers last month? To which customers? For which products? If you can't answer in 30 seconds, you're overpaying. Scanerrific logs every dispatch with courier cost, tracking number, and recipient — giving you real visibility into your sample spend.

  • Recipient history for faster re-orders
